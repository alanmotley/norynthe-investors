Website privacy practices.

This page summarizes how Norynthe uses basic website analytics, contact information, and limited investor access data across Norynthe web properties.

Scope

This privacy notice applies to Norynthe web properties, including the public website at www.norynthe.com and investor-facing pages operated by Norynthe.

The sites are primarily informational. They are used to explain Norynthe, provide public company materials, support selected investor review, and allow people to contact Norynthe.

What We Collect

Norynthe may collect the following categories of information:

  • Information you choose to send, such as form submissions, emails, meeting requests, diligence questions, or other messages sent to Norynthe.
  • Basic website usage data, such as page views, referring pages, approximate location, device/browser information, scrolls, outbound clicks, file downloads, and similar interaction data.
  • Investor-site interaction signals, such as material opens, review track clicks, access unlock events, and follow-up request clicks.
  • Local browser state used by the investor portal to remember access status during review and to avoid double-counting first-open events during a session.

Norynthe does not intentionally send names, email addresses, passwords, private investor documents, or message contents to Google Analytics.

Norynthe currently uses Tally to collect website form submissions.

Analytics

Norynthe uses Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors move through the sites, which materials are opened, and whether visitors use contact or follow-up links.

Analytics events are used in aggregate to improve the website, clarify investor review paths, and understand which pages or materials are most useful.

Google provides more information about Google Analytics privacy controls and the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries, investor requests, and follow-up conversations.
  • To understand which public and investor materials are most useful.
  • To improve page structure, navigation, messaging, and site performance.
  • To keep investor materials lightly access-controlled and understand review behavior at a high level.
  • To maintain the security, reliability, and integrity of Norynthe web properties.

Sharing

Norynthe does not sell personal information. Norynthe may share limited information with service providers that help operate the websites, analytics, hosting, email, contact forms, or related business systems.

Norynthe may also disclose information if required by law, to protect rights or safety, or in connection with a business transaction involving Norynthe.

Choices

  • You can use browser settings to block or delete cookies.
  • You can use Google's Analytics opt-out browser add-on where available.
  • You can contact Norynthe at hello@norynthe.com with privacy questions.
This notice is a practical summary of current Norynthe website practices. It may be updated as the websites, analytics setup, or contact workflows change.
