Capital Deployment

The raise is modeled around durable infrastructure before payroll scale.

This model prioritizes fully owned operating infrastructure, evaluation capacity, legal setup, and runway. Instead of using the initial capital primarily for headcount, the budget is structured around the operating base required for independent AI evaluation.

Runway Logic

Founder-led execution preserves optionality until enterprise demand is validated.

The model assumes disciplined early operating costs and prioritizes runway preservation until enterprise demand, pilot feedback, and product-market signals justify expansion.

Operating Mode

Founder-led operations

One employee during the initial phase, with limited payroll burden before validation.

Infrastructure

Fully owned compute

Capital goes toward evaluation control and larger-model testing on upgraded compute.

Cost Discipline

Controlled software and cloud costs

The model avoids cloud-heavy experimentation consuming the raise early.

Workflow Leverage

AI-assisted operations

Commercial Trigger

Pilots before hiring

Scale Gate

Expand from evidence

Commercial Milestones

Scale follows infrastructure, larger-model testing, and enterprise validation.

Phase 1

Infrastructure foundation

Secure infrastructure, compute, legal structure, and operating base.

Phase 2

Larger-model testing

Push the Norynthe MVP OS to upgraded compute and tune the already-built scoring and evidence framework against larger-model performance.

Phase 3

Enterprise engagement

Engage procurement, governance, risk, compliance, and enterprise AI stakeholders.

Phase 4

Controlled pilots

Run pilot programs and validate paid access use cases.

Phase 5

Validated packaging

Convert validated use cases into subscription, custom evaluation, or advisory revenue.

Forecast Treatment

The page shows modeled upside, not guaranteed outcomes.

Forecast figures are assumptions. Revenue development depends on customer discovery, procurement stakeholder validation, pilot conversion, pricing acceptance, delivery capacity, and enterprise willingness to pay for independent AI evaluation and trust-scoring infrastructure. Profitability is modeled as an operating scenario output, not a guaranteed profitability promise.
Investor Interpretation

Infrastructure first. Revenue modeled from validation.

The raise is not designed to immediately maximize headcount. It is designed to give Norynthe enough independence, compute capacity, legal structure, and runway to build credible AI evaluation infrastructure before scaling.
